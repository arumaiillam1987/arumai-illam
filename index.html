About Us

Arumai Illam is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing shelter, food, and care for elderly individuals who are abandoned or in need. Our mission is to ensure dignity, love, and support for every resident.

Our Mission

  • Provide safe shelter for elderly people
  • Offer food, healthcare, and emotional support
  • Create a loving and respectful environment
