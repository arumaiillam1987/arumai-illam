Welcome to Arumai Illam

Arumai Illam is a registered charitable society located in Chozhanpettai, Mayiladuthurai, Tamil Nadu. Founded in 1987, Arumai Illam has been a beacon of care and compassion for the most vulnerable members of society — particularly senior citizens, destitutes, and intellectually disabled children.

Our work is rooted in the belief that every human being deserves dignity, love, and the right to live in a safe, nurturing environment, regardless of their age, ability, or background.

Our Core Services

Home for Senior Citizens

A peaceful and secure home for abandoned elderly individuals and destitutes.

  • Comfortable shelter and daily care
  • Nutritious meals
  • Medical attention and regular check-ups
  • Companionship and emotional support
  • Loving and respectful environment

School of Hope for Intellectually Disabled

A dedicated special school focusing on care-based education and skill-building.

  • Basic academic learning
  • Social and emotional development
  • Daily living skills (hygiene, communication)
  • Individual attention and customized plans

Our Belief

  • Compassion – Love and care can heal beyond medicine
  • Dignity – Every life deserves respect
  • Commitment – Serving the neglected with honesty and hope

Arumai Illam is more than just a home or a school — it is a family for those who have none, and a lifeline for those who are often forgotten.
